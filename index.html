Pancakes

Pancake is a type of flat pastry, usually thin and round, prepared with a starch-based slurry containing eggs, milk and butter.

Cupcakes

Cupcake is a type of small cake prepared to serve a person and is usually baked in a small, thin paper or aluminum round cake tin. As with large cakes, decorations, sauces, etc. added to the cake. It is also common in cupcakes.

Hummus

Hummus is a Middle Eastern appetizer made with chickpeas and tahini by adding lemon juice, garlic, salt, cumin, red pepper and olive oil. It is also written and read as Houmous and Hummus. Hummus means chickpea in Arabic.

Hamburger

Hamburger, or simply Burger, is a type of sandwich made with a patty placed between two slices of round sandwich bread. It is usually served with the addition of different ingredients such as ketchup, mayonnaise, pickles, onions, tomatoes, mustard and so on.

Salmon

Salmon is a common forage fish classified as an oily fish with its rich protein and omega-3 fatty acids content.

Vegetables

A vegetable is the name given to the parts of plants that are eaten by humans or other animals. The term vegetable can mean different things between cultures and cuisines.
